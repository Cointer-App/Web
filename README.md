## Overview

This is the web app for Cointer. It lets you watch your crypto addresses and manage your notifications from any browser. no accounts or passwords needed.

<br />

## Features

<table>
<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

**Login:**
Paste in an existing key or create a new one. New keys are shown once, with the option to copy or download them before continuing.

</td>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

**Dashboard:**
See your totals for the last day, week, and month. Shows your list of watched addresses, recent deposits, and a breakdown by asset.

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

**Activity Page:**
Full history of deposits, priced in your currency, with pagination.

</td>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

**Channels Page:**
Add and manage notification channels like ntfy, Discord, Slack, and email. Turn channels on or off, and send a test alert.

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

**Settings Page:**
Reveal, copy, or rotate your personal key. View and remove devices registered for push notifications.

</td>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

**Themes:**
Switch between light, dark, and system theme. Your choice is saved for next time.

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

**Sidebar Navigation:**
Simple sidebar to move between Dashboard, Activity, Channels, and Settings.

</td>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

**Delete Account:**
Wipe your key and everything tied to it. Requires typing a confirmation so it does not happen by accident.

</td>
</tr>
</table>
